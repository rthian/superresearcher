# SuperResearcher -- Self-Serve Pilot Guide

Welcome to SuperResearcher! This guide helps you navigate the customer insights platform as a pilot user.

---

## Getting Started

### Access

Open the SuperResearcher UI in your browser:

```
http://[server-address]:3000
```

If token auth is enabled, append your token:
```
http://[server-address]:3000?token=your-viewer-token
```

---

## What You Can Do

### Browse Insights

Navigate to **Insights** in the sidebar. You can:
- View all extracted customer insights across projects
- Filter by project, impact level, category, or product area
- Click any insight to see full evidence and source quotes

### View Actions

Navigate to **Actions**. You can:
- See all prioritized action items generated from insights
- Filter by priority (Critical, High, Medium, Low) and department
- View success metrics and linked insights for each action

### CSAT & NPS Dashboard

Navigate to **CSAT & NPS**. You can:
- See bank-wide and per-organization CSAT scores
- View NPS trends over time (QoQ, YoY)
- See alerts for metric drops
- Review dimensional CSAT (design, trust, security, etc.)

### ROI Tracker

Navigate to **ROI Tracker**. You can:
- See which actions have been tracked against CSAT/NPS outcomes
- View before/after metric deltas per action
- See aggregate impact across all tracked actions

### Competitive Intelligence

Navigate to **Competitive Intel**. You can:
- **Feature Matrix** -- See which features GXS/GXB have vs competitors
- **Pricing** -- View interest rate and pricing changes over time
- **Releases** -- See competitor feature release timeline
- **Perception** -- View customer perception themes (positive/negative)

### Projects

Navigate to **Projects**. You can:
- Browse all research projects (App Store VoC, surveys, qualitative studies)
- View study details, metadata, and methodology

### Personas

Navigate to **Personas**. You can:
- View customer personas derived from research
- See supporting insights for each persona

### Search

Press **Cmd+K** (Mac) to open global search. Search across projects, insights, and actions.

---

## What You Cannot Do (Read-Only Mode)

As a pilot user, the following are restricted:
- Upload new data (CSAT, surveys, app store reviews)
- Edit or delete insights or actions
- Modify project settings
- Update personas

These are managed by the Customer Insights team. If you need changes, reach out to them.

## FAQ

**Q: How often is data updated?**
A: App store reviews are ingested monthly. CSAT/NPS is updated monthly. Competitive data is updated as events occur.

**Q: Can I export data?**
A: Yes, most pages support export. Look for export buttons on tables and charts.

**Q: Who generates the insights?**
A: Insights are extracted from raw data (app store reviews, survey verbatims, interview transcripts) using a combination of AI and human curation.

**Q: How do I request a new research study?**
A: Contact the Customer Insights team with your research question and business objective.
