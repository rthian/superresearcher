## Business Objective

This quarterly Voice of Customer study measures overall customer sentiment, satisfaction, and brand health across both GXS and GXB digital banking platforms. The study aims to:

1. **Track Performance:** Establish quarterly baseline metrics for customer love, satisfaction, and Net Promoter Score (NPS)
2. **Identify Trends:** Compare performance quarter-over-quarter to detect improvements or declines
3. **Guide Prioritization:** Surface actionable insights and recommendations to inform product roadmap and operational decisions
4. **Benchmark Competition:** Compare GXS and GXB performance to identify strengths, weaknesses, and learning opportunities
5. **Drive Action:** Generate concrete recommendations for product, engineering, customer support, and marketing teams

This study directly informs quarterly business reviews, OKR planning, and resource allocation decisions.

---

## Research Questions

### Primary Questions

1. **Customer Love & Loyalty**
   - What is the overall customer satisfaction (CSAT) for GXS and GXB?
   - What is the Net Promoter Score (NPS) for each bank?
   - How likely are customers to recommend our banks to friends and family?
   - What drives customer loyalty and retention?

2. **Product Experience**
   - Which product features are most valued by customers?
   - What are the top pain points and friction areas in the user journey?
   - How does the mobile app experience compare to customer expectations?
   - Are there feature gaps compared to traditional banks or competitors?

3. **Service Quality**
   - How satisfied are customers with customer support responsiveness and quality?
   - What are the most common support issues and resolution times?
   - How do customers rate the onboarding and account setup experience?

4. **Competitive Position**
   - How do customers compare our banks to traditional banks (DBS, OCBC, UOB)?
   - What are perceived advantages of GXS/GXB vs. competitors?
   - What would cause customers to switch to another bank?

### Secondary Questions

5. Are there demographic or behavioral differences in satisfaction levels?
6. What emerging needs or use cases are customers requesting?
7. How has customer sentiment changed since last quarter?
8. Which channels (app, web, support) need immediate attention?

## Methodology

### Data Collection Approach

**Multi-Source VOC Collection:**

1. **App Store Reviews** (Ongoing)
   - iOS App Store and Google Play Store reviews
   - Sentiment analysis of ratings and comments
   - Trend analysis of feature mentions

2. **In-App Surveys** (Monthly/Quarterly)
   - CSAT surveys after key interactions
   - NPS surveys at regular intervals
   - Feature-specific feedback forms

3. **Customer Support Tickets** (Ongoing)
   - Analysis of support conversation themes
   - Resolution time and quality metrics
   - Common issues and complaints

4. **User Interviews** (Quarterly)
   - 15-20 in-depth interviews per bank
   - Mix of promoters, passives, and detractors
   - 45-minute structured interviews

5. **Social Media Monitoring** (Ongoing)
   - Facebook, Instagram, Twitter mentions
   - Reddit discussions
   - Banking forums and review sites

## Target Participants

### GXS Bank

- **Segment:** Active GXS retail banking customers
- **Criteria:** 
  - Account active for 30+ days
  - Completed at least 3 transactions
  - Mix of high-balance and average users
  - Age range: 21-65
  - Mix of tech-savvy and digitally cautious users
- **Target Count:** 500+ survey responses, 10 interviews per quarter
- **NPS Distribution Target:** 30% Promoters, 40% Passives, 30% Detractors

### GXB Bank

- **Segment:** Active GXB retail and business banking customers
- **Criteria:**
  - Account active for 30+ days
  - Completed at least 3 transactions
  - Include both personal and business account holders
  - Age range: 25-65
  - Mix of SME owners and individual customers
- **Target Count:** 500+ survey responses, 10 interviews per quarter
- **NPS Distribution Target:** 30% Promoters, 40% Passives, 30% Detractors
