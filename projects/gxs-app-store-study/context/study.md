## Business Objective

Analyze GXS Bank app store reviews from iOS App Store and Google Play Store (Q1-Q2 2025) to identify user pain points, unmet needs, and opportunities for product improvement.

---

## Research Questions

1. What are the primary pain points causing negative reviews?
2. What features are users requesting most frequently?
3. What aspects of the app do users appreciate most?
4. What critical bugs are affecting user experience?

---

## Target Participants

- **Segment:** GXS Bank mobile app users (Singapore market)
- **Criteria:** Users who left app store reviews in Q1-Q2 2025
- **Sample Size:** 150+ reviews across iOS and Android platforms

---

## Key Findings

### Critical Issues (Immediate Action Required)
1. **Missing Digital Wallet Support** - Users cannot add cards to Apple Pay/Google Pay, a baseline expectation in 2025
2. **Account Freezes Without Warning** - Accounts blocked with no notification, multi-week review periods with no communication
3. **Customer Support Failures** - 2+ hour wait times, chat disconnects, unhelpful resolutions
4. **Bank Linking Bug** - Dropdown showing no banks, blocking new user onboarding

### High-Impact Pain Points
5. **Hidden Withdrawal Limits** - Users discover 10K daily limits and 12-hour cooling periods after depositing funds
6. **Declining Interest Rates** - Rates dropped from 2.8% to 1.98%, causing user exodus concerns
7. **Poor Fraud Handling** - Disputed transactions not resolved, users report to MAS

### Positive Differentiators
8. **Intuitive UX** - Interface praised as "miles ahead of incumbents"
9. **Pockets Feature** - Savings organization feature highly valued
10. **Fast Transactions** - Speed appreciated vs. traditional banks
